Itinerary for Varanasi

(Budget 5k-7k)/Person

Varanasi

>> Regarded as the spiritual capital of India, the city draws Hindu pilgrims


>> It is also famous for its trade and commerce, especially for the finest silks and gold.


Saranath

>> It is a great pilgrimage centre for Buddhist.


>> Sarnath is the place where Buddha preached his first sermon after enlightenment in Bodh Gaya.

Dashashwamedh Ghat

>>Dashashwamedh Ghat is a main ghat in Varanasi on the Ganga River in Uttar Pradesh.


>> The aarti takes place daily at 6:30 PM post sundown in summer and 7:00 PM in winters for about 45 minutes.
