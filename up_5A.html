Itinerary for Agra And Vrindavan

(Budget 10k-12k)/Person

Taj Mahal

>> Mehtab Bagh in the first half of the day


>> Then take an entire tour of the majestic Taj Mahal.


Agra Fort

>> Agra Fort are a mixture of different architectural styles.


>>Jahangiri Mahal is the most important building of the Akbari period in the Agra Fort

Agra Market

>> Agra is best known for its temples, forts and local markets


>> A person who comes to Agra is unlikely to return empty-handed for there are so many arts, crafts, and other souvenir shops.

Mathura Vrindavan

>> Mathura Vrindavan isfamous for the ancient temples of Lord Krishna.

>> It is the primary site of pilgrimage for an annual celebration of Janmashtamihna.


Prem Mandir

>> Vrindavan has about 5,500 temples


>> It is also afamous centre of Buddhism in ancient India.
