Itinerary for Kerala

(Budget 14k-16k)/Person

Alleppey

>> There is the whole of Kerala in one side, and then there is this heavenly tourist destination called Alappuzha


>> Alleppey is known for its silent backwaters and bountiful beauty!


Cochin

>>Cochin is a unique placein God’s Own Country that is perfectly blended with cultural values and modern ideas!


Fondly called the ‘Queen of the Arabian Sea’, this imposing port-city has always been an attraction point

Kollam

>> A quaint port-city in God’s Own Country, Kollam has its own charm and appeal.


>> Enthralled by the turquoise Lakshadweep Sea and caressed by the emerald Ashtamudi Lake

Kovalam

>> If you are a beach goer, and is still assuming none can beat the charm Goa, you must visit Kovalam today!


>> Located along the coastlines of the charismatic Arabian Sea


Kumarakom

>> Adorned by the magical charm of the Vembanad Lake, Kumarakom is another beguiling backwater destination.


>> Kumarakom is popular among all the types of visitors.

Wayanad

>> Cradled in the lap of the Western Ghats, Wayanad truly deserves a top rank in the list


>> Situated at an astounding height of 700-2,100m, this stunning hill station is all about fantasy,


Thekkady

>> Considering that you want more from your Kerala holidays, you should not miss a trip to Thekkady,


>>The ‘Spice Capital of Kerala’, Thekkady is much popular as the natural abode to numerous exotic wildlife.

Vagamon

>> If you are looking for an offbeat, yet captivating holiday experience in Kerala, you must plan your vacation in Vagamon.


>> In addition to leisure travellers, thrill and adventure seekers also make it to Vagamon in a considerable number.
